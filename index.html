Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Hair Lounge located in Kuwait?

Hair Lounge is located in Salmiya, Block 10, Issa Al Qatami Street, Building No. 38, 2nd Floor, opposite Wahed Kabab Restaurant, Kuwait City. We are easy to find and conveniently situated in one of Salmiya's most vibrant neighbourhoods.

What are your opening hours?

Hair Lounge is open every day of the week from 9AM to 9PM, including weekends and public holidays. Walk-ins are always welcome, or book in advance via WhatsApp at +965 411 65200.

What beauty services do you offer?

We offer a full range of beauty and wellness services: haircut, hair coloring, hair treatments, facials, Hydra Facial, threading, waxing, pedicure, manicure, gel polish, nail art, nail extensions, acrylic nails, Moroccan bath, relaxation massage, western massage, Ayurvedic massage, and professional makeup.

How do I book an appointment?

Booking is simple! Send a WhatsApp message to +965 411 65200 or call us directly. You can also walk in during our opening hours (9AM–9PM daily) and our friendly team will take care of you right away.

Do you offer Moroccan bath and massage services?

Yes! Hair Lounge offers authentic Moroccan Bath (Hammam) with full-body scrub and deep cleansing, as well as Relaxation Massage, Western Massage, and Ayurvedic Massage with aromatic herbal oils. We recommend booking these in advance via WhatsApp to ensure availability.
